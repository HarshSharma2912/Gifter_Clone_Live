How can I use my Gift Voucher ?

Not only are our vouchers delivered instantly,they are also very easy to redeem. A voucher that can be used at a store simply needs to be presented at the cash counter at the time of billing. The voucher amount is adjusted and the remaining amount of the bill, if any, will need to be paid in cash/card. Please note that these are one-time use vouchers. To make the most of it, try using vouchers if the billing value is equal to or more than the voucher amount.
